Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Expire Product ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the application, we may collect the following information:

  • Your Google account information (only when you choose to sign in).
  • Your product information, including product names, expiration dates, notes, and reminder settings.
  • Cloud backup data that you choose to synchronize.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment information, contacts, SMS messages, photos, or your precise location.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used only to provide application features, including:

  • Synchronizing your data across multiple devices.
  • Backing up and restoring your data.
  • Providing expiration reminders.
  • Improving application stability and reliability.

3. Cloud Backup

If you sign in with your Google account, your application data may be securely stored using Google Cloud services. This allows your information to be restored if you change or replace your device.

Only the information required for synchronization is stored.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your information is only processed by trusted service providers that are necessary to operate the application, such as Google Cloud services.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account directly within the application. When your account is deleted:

  • Your cloud backup data is permanently removed.
  • Your synchronization information is deleted.
  • The deletion cannot be undone.

Deleting your account does not delete your Google Account.

7. Children's Privacy

Expire Product is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
