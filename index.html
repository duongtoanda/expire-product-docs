About Expire Product

Expire Product is an application designed to help users manage expiration dates for food, medicine, cosmetics, and other daily-use products.

The app helps you keep track of product expiration dates, receive reminders, back up your data, and restore it when switching devices.

  • Track product expiration dates
  • Receive reminders before products expire
  • Back up and restore data using Google Cloud
  • Delete your account and associated cloud data directly in the app
