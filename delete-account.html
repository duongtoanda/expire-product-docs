Delete Account

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Expire Product allows you to permanently delete your account directly within the application. Deleting your account will permanently remove your cloud data associated with Expire Product and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Expire Product.
  2. Sign in with your Google account (if you are not already signed in).
  3. Navigate to Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Read the warning message carefully.
  6. Confirm the deletion.

What Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Your cloud backup data.
  • Your synchronized application data.
  • Your account information stored by Expire Product.
  • Any future synchronization associated with your account.

What Will NOT Be Deleted

  • Your Google Account.
  • Data stored locally on your device if you uninstall the application without deleting your account first.
  • Information stored outside of Expire Product.

Data Retention

Once your deletion request is confirmed, your cloud data is permanently deleted. This process cannot be reversed, and deleted data cannot be recovered.
